How to Get Started

Trading these tokenized commodities is straightforward and fully on-chain via the XRPL decentralized exchange (DEX). Here's the simple process:

Step1

  • Explore the Commodities Section
    Browse the table and find the token you're interested in (e.g., XAG for silver).
    Click the "Buy/Sell" button for that token.
    This will take you directly to the Ripple Ledger DEX trading platform where the token is on offered against XRP.

Step2

  • Trade on the DEX
    Connect your XRPL-compatible wallet (e.g., Xaman, Ledger, Gem, or any supporting wallet).
    Enter the amount of the token (or XRP) you want to swap.
    Review the details (price, fees ≈0.0002 XRP per tx, any slippage), then confirm the swap.
    Done! The trade settles in 3 - 5 seconds - fully transparent and on-chain.
